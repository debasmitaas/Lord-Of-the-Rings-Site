Choose your Favourite characters as avatars!!

Frodo Baggins

The brave and determined Ring-bearer tasked with destroying the One Ring. Frodo's journey from the Shire to Mount Doom tests his strength, courage, and resilience.

Samwise Gamgee

Frodo's loyal and steadfast friend. Sam's unwavering dedication, bravery, and practical nature make him an invaluable companion on the quest to destroy the Ring.

Merry Brandybuck

A clever and adventurous hobbit from Buckland. Merry's quick thinking and bravery play crucial roles in aiding the Fellowship and rallying the Riders of Rohan.

Pippin Took

The mischievous and curious hobbit who grows into a courageous and resourceful warrior. Pippin's journey from the Shire to Gondor showcases his transformation and bravery.

Gandalf The Grey

A wise and powerful wizard, known as Gandalf the Grey and later as Gandalf the White. He is a guide and protector to the Fellowship, using his knowledge and magic to combat the forces of darkness.

Legolas

An Elven prince from Mirkwood known for his unparalleled archery skills and keen senses. As a member of the Fellowship, Legolas demonstrates agility, grace, and loyalty, forging a deep friendship with the dwarf Gimli.

Gimli

A brave and stalwart dwarf warrior, son of Glóin. Gimli's strength and combat prowess are invaluable to the Fellowship. His friendship with Legolas bridges the gap between elves and dwarves.

Aragorn

The rightful heir to the throne of Gondor, also known as Strider. Aragorn is a skilled ranger and warrior with a noble heart and a deep sense of duty. His leadership and bravery are crucial in the fight against Sauron.
